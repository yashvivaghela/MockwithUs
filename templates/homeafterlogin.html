What is Interview Assistant?

For a job seeker, job interviews can be stressful, especially if not feeling fully ready for the interview. A mock interview can be useful to reduce the stress of interview process before actually appearing for interviews in front of companies. A mock interview is a kind of simulation of an actual job interview. The mock interview provides practice for a job seeker before having a real-world job interview. In this project we are making a website in which a person can give a mock interview and by analyzing expression based on face and speech of the candidate using features of machine learning,feedbacks will be given.
